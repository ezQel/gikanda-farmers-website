Welcome to Gikanda Coffee
Farmers Cooperative Society

At Gikanda Coffee, we are dedicated to crafting exceptional coffee from
the heart of Kenya to coffee lovers in the rest of the World. Our
cooperative society, Gikanda Coffee Farmers Cooperative Society,
represents a community of passionate coffee farmers who take
immense pride in their craft.

Join Our Journey

We invite you to discover the passion and dedication that go into every cup of Gikanda Coffee. From our farms to your cup, we are proud to share the flavors of Mount Kenya with the world. Together, we are not just brewing coffee; we are fostering a tradition, a community, and a commitment to a better future.
Thank you for being a part of our story!

Why Choose
Gikanda Coffee

  • Pure Terroir
    Our coffee cherishes its birthplace, thriving on the unique terroir created by Mount Kenya's majestic presence and nourished by the vibrant red volcanic soils.
  • Premium Quality
    Gikanda Coffee is celebrated for its exquisite flavor profile, with notes that dance on your palate.
  • Sustainability & Heritage
    We are committed to sustainable farming practices that not only enhance our coffee but also protect our environment. We're not just coffee growers; we're guardians of tradition and stewards of the environment, ensuring each cup of Gikanda Coffee represents a sustainable legacy.
  • Empowering Communities
    By choosing Gikanda Coffee, you directly contribute to the welfare and prosperity of local farmers.
  • Certified Excellence
    Discover coffee with organic and Fair Trade certifications, a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality and ethics. Discover Our Latest Updates
  • New Coffee Varieties
    Explore our newest coffee varieties, carefully nurtured and handpicked for your enjoyment.
  • Community Engagement
    Learn about our ongoing initiatives to uplift the lives of our community members.
  • Exclusive Offer
    For a limited time, enjoy special pricing on our premium coffee beans. Come, journey with us as we unveil the remarkable story behind every cup of Gikanda Coffee. From the mountain's gentle slopes, the sun-drenched coffee fields, to our meticulous roasting process, our devotion to excellence is unwavering. Join us in savoring the extraordinary, supporting sustainable agriculture, and celebrating the pure essence of Kenyan coffee.
Thank you for choosing Gikanda Coffee—the epitome of nature's beauty in a cup.
