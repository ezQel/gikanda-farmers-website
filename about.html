About Us

At Gikanda Coffee, our journey is a testament to the dedication, tradition, and passion that run deep in the heart of Kenyan coffee farming. Our story begins in the lush, fertile soils surrounding the iconic Mount Kenya, where our cooperative society, Gikanda Coffee Farmers Cooperative Society, was founded.

Our Roots

Established in 1996, our cooperative has been nurturing the art of coffee cultivation for generations. Our founders, inspired by the rich terroir of our region, set out to craft coffee that would captivate the world with its flavor and character.

We are committed to the following principles...

  • Quality Excellence
    We meticulously nurture and harvest our coffee cherries to ensure every cup of Gikanda Coffee reflects the essence of our terroir.
  • Sustainability
    We champion eco-friendly farming practices that safeguard the environment for future generations.
  • Community Upliftment
    Our cooperative stands as a beacon of hope, improving the livelihoods of our farmers and their families.
  • Global Responsibility
    We proudly hold certifications in organic and fair trade practices, demonstrating our commitment to global ethical standards.
